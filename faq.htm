What happens if the weather is bad

You will be either a refund or to reschedule. It's your choice.

Transfers

Transfers are not included. If you require a transfer to/from hotel/airport, we can recommend a local taxi operator that we had used. They are very professional and reliable. Contact us for details.

Wi-Fi

Wi-Fi is available on board (subject to coastal signal strength).

Use of sails

The use of sails is not guaranteed. This is done to respect itinerary.
