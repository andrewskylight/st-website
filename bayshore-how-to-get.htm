How to get to Bayshore marina

What do I bring

  • Towels
  • Cash (Pig Beach Interactions: $30 - $35 per person; and various purchases on board; USD and BSD are equivalent)
  • You can bring your own lunch, drinks, and alcoholic drinks (onboard lunch and rum & coke are included in the tour; but feel free to bring your own stuff)

How do I get there

The following link shows the exact location of the boat in the marina (switch to satellite view for details):

Phoenix 777 at Bayshore Marina

Step 1: Get to the marina

We depart from Bayshore Marina (not Baystreet Marina; that's a different place)

64 E Bay St, Nassau

By taxi

You are heading to Bayshore Marina (not Baystreet). Bayshore marina is on Bay street "after the bridge" just pass Poop deck and before Rubis / Harbour club. The entrance is on the left.

By car

Please use this plaza as a reference. Guests had successfully parked their cars there.

Then walk to Bayshore Marina and ask for "Phoenix" at the security gate.

Step 2: Get to the boat

Tell the security guard you are here for "Phoenix" and they will show you where to go.

    In the event a security guard:

  • asks for a slip number: you are in a wrong place. Return to Bay St (main street) and follow the traffic. Entrance to the Bayshore marina will be on the left
  • says you are at Nassau Yacht Heaven. Return to Bay St (main street) and go against traffic flow. Entrance to the Bayshore marina will be on the right
  • says you are at Baystreet marina (not the same as Bayshore Marina). Follow the traffic on Bay St. Bayshore is 9min away by car or 15min away by foot
