Kite Surfing in Cuba

Let's have fun together...

Scroll down to learn more

Sailing and Kite surfing - satisfy your wind cravings.

Sail Tomorrow invites you to explore stunning Canarreos Archipelago, Cuba. During this unique experience you will enjoy two days under sail and four days of unforgettable kitesurfing experience.

Tour: Cayo Largo

Itinerary:

Day 1 (Sunday)
Departure Cienfuegos. Bay of Cienfuegos, panoramic view of Jaqua fort, hypnotizing views of Caribbean Seas, sunset and a sunset under sail.
Days 2-5 (Mon-Thu)
Kite Surfying Cayo Largo. Live on the boat. Explore the surroounding islands, and beaches, including Playa Sirena, which stays cold even during the hottest summer days, due to it's unique composition. 3 snacks per day on board, and a friendly crew. Those who are outgoing can spend their time at a local bar or check out local night life.
Day 6 (Friday)
Arrival Cienfuegos late at night.
