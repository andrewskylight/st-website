The best of Cayman Islands ... under sail

Sail Tomorrow invites you to explore Cayman Islands. This is a new tour being offered at a special reduced rate, so hurry up and jump on board.

Tour: Cayman Islands

Itinerary:

Embarkation

Greeting from a friendly skipper. Boarding of the vessel. Mandatory safety briefing.

Coral Garden

Half day in the water, half day on deck sunbathing. An easy going fun adventure that will get you wet on the first day, give you a taste of the salt water, and smoooth your transition into holidays.

Stingray Sandbar

Situated in a shallow 10-15 foot sandbar, Stingray City may exceed imagination of even of the most creative one. This is a wonderful place, visited by thirty friendly stingrays every day.

Turtle Swim

Seven Mile Beach

Everyone needs some down time ... and away from the boat time. A seven mile beach is a perfect place for that. Here you can just ... do nothing and feel good about it, for as long as you want.

Reef Snorkel

Back into water for a long snorkel along a shoreline. This will be a great demonstration of fish behaviour in the water. After a few minutes you will notice that smaller fish will treat you like a bigger one: some will run away, some will tag along, becoming your little escort.

Shipwreck Snorkel

A tiny boat accessible by snorkel is a nice change of scenery from flora and fauna. Especially, for those technical ones.

Debarkation

Relaxed, refreshed and rejuvenated you will disembark. And while you will leave our vessel behind, the memories of this voyage will stay with you for the rest of your life.
