Booking Options

Sail Tomorrow is proud to provide a variety of options to deliver the best vacation experience for your customers. Our tours split into sailing and diving options. These options are flexible. Learn more about what's included in all of our sailing and diving trips below.

Travel For A Day, Week, Or More

Daily and weekly tours available with a base itinerary. Contact us if you desire to travel for a different length of time. We've accommodate guests who have embarked for 4 day and even 12 day trips. Our staff will work with you to organize the trip around your desired dates and needs.

Individuals, Groups, and Exclusive Yacht Charter Available

Individual, groups, and exclusive yacht charter bookings welcome. We accommodate up to 8 passengers on our yacht for both sailing and diving trips.

All-Inclusive

Each trip includes the following benefits

  • Three meals per day. Snacks, beverages, and alcohol (rum and beer)
  • Airport transfer to the marina from Havana, Varadero, and Santa Clara airports
  • Dedicated skipper and mariner on board
  • Room cleaning and toiletries
  • Yacht fuel provided in case of poor wind conditions

Custom Tailored To You

Senior travel consultants work with you to make sure your vacation meets your needs and desires. Looking for access to a specific show, beach, or island? We will organize it to be a part of your trip.

Sail Tomorrow has a large and experienced network of partners on the ground to research and arrange a custom itinerary. Guests in the past have enjoyed private tours in Havana, Cienfuegos, and Trinidad, underwater fishing, and relaxation on a private beach.

Sailing Tours

On sailing tours, guests are able to spend time exploring Cuba while taking breaks and traveling on the sailing yacht. Depending on the length of the trip, sailing tours will dock in Cienfuegos, Cayo Largo, and Trinidad. Each location has something unique to offer.

In the past, guests have tasted delicious Cuban cuisine at the top restaurants in the region, attended shows, participated in historical tours, relaxed on a remote beach, or snorkeled.

At your request, each trip can include snorkeling and fishing equipment at no additional cost. We will also work with our partners in Cuba to arrange any tours or shows you desire.

Diving Tours

Sail Tomorrow's Vision III vessel has been completely renovated with divers in mind. Divers can expect between 2-4 dives per day on each trip. We provide diving equipment, oxygenated tanks, weights to all of our diving guests at no additional cost. As well, our expert divemaster, Israel, who is on board every trip, will show you the best dive sites in the area.

Included For All Divers

  • 2-4 dives per day.
  • Dive sites for beginner to master level.
  • Dedicated divemaster on board to guide you
  • Compressor on board to re-oxygenate tanks
  • Diving equipment (mask, fin, BCD, regulator, and weights)

Meet Our Divemaster: Israel

A Cuban native, Israel has over 10 years of experience diving in the region. Before working with Sail Tomorrow, he supported many other well-known yacht charters, such as Amazing Enterprise.

Fluent in English and Spanish, Israel plans a custom dive itinerary for all of our guests. Each itinerary is fitted to the experience and interests each diver. He also participates in each dive to guide you through the site.
