Discover Cuban Culture

Our trusted and experienced guides will make your visit to Cuba and introduction to it's culture, flora and fuana unforgettable

Itinerary:

December 18: Havana

  • Pick up in Havana Airport.
  • Check in Hotel or Casa Particular Havana.
  • Lunch or Snack Time.
  • City Tour Old Havana.

    • Walking tour of the Historic Center of Old Havana (UNESCO Cultural World Heritage Site), San Francisco de Asis Square, Plaza Vieja. Return along Mercaderes Street to the hotel Ambos Mundos with a visit to the room where used to rest the American writer Ernest Hemingway, Plaza de Armas, Cathedral Square and panoramic tour to bar-restaurant La Bodeguita del Medio.

  • Dinner
  • Free Night

    • Optional: Visit el Morro for the cannon shot ceremony “El Cañonazo de las 9”

December 19: Viñales

  • Breakfast
  • 08:00 Departure to Viñales in Pinar del Rio.

    • Viñales is one of the most beautiful places in Cuba, declared UNESCO Natural World Heritage Site have a unique formation of mountains over the fields of Tabaco, sightsee the Prehistory Mural which are paintings over the oldest rock in the island, but Pinar de Rio and Viñales are also famous for having the best Tabaco in the Word, the atmosphere, and of course the experience of many generations of Masters Vegueros (Tabaco Farmers).

  • Stop at Viewpoint in the hotel Los Jasmines.
  • Visit one vega (Tabaco Farm) to know about the process of the cultivation, drying, fermentation and final preparation of Tabaco (optional: degustation of the Tabaco from this vega).
  • Horse tour for the Viñales Valley.
  • Lunch at Private Restaurant “El Campesino”.
  • Visit the Indian Cave including a boat tour of its underground river.
  • Sightsee the Prehistory Mural which are paintings over the oldest rock in the island.
  • Back to Havana.
  • Dinner.
  • Free Night

    • Optional: Dinner and Show at El Guajirito for live traditional Cuban music with musician of Buena Vista Social Club and African All Star projects).

December 20: Cienaga de Zapata – Bay of Pigs

  • Breakfast.
  • 08:00 Departure to Playa Girón.
  • Meeting whit the nature at Crocodile Farm.

    • Mini-zoo with species in extinction like Iguanas, Manjuari, Cuban Tree Rat and Cuban Crocodile.

  • Lunch Time (special degustation with exotic Cuban plates).
  • Snorkeling and swimming in the Fish Cave.

    • One of the most beautiful places in Cuba for these activities, natural aquarium.

  • Departure to Cienfuegos.
  • Check in Particular House Cienfuegos.
  • Dinner.
  • Free Night.

    • Optional: visit to Cabaret El Cubanisimo for Cuban Show and recorder music.

December 21: Cienfuegos

  • Breakfast.
  • City Tour Cienfuegos

    • Cultural World Heritage Site: walking in the Cienfuegos boulevard, visit to the House of Rum and Tabaco, Union Hotel arriving to the foundation area the Jose Martí square, the oldest part in the city with a view of the main buildings, the Town Hall, the Cathedral, Tomas Terry theater, Ferrer Palace, The Arc of the Workers.

  • Lunch or Snack time.
  • Sailing Tour “Sunset from the bay” aboard yacht “Vision III”.
  • Panoramic Cienfuegos.

    • Tour through Prado promenade (the largest in Cuba), Cienfuegos seawall(Malecón), Club Cienfuegos, Park of the Sculptures, Jagua Hotel, Valley Palace.

  • Dinner in Private Restaurant
  • Visit to Cabaret Tropisur to enjoy a special Cuban Show Tropicana Style.

December 22: El Nicho

  • Breakfast.
  • Safari to “El Nicho”.

    • Located in the mountains of Escambray the national park “El Nicho” is an ideal place to enjoy beautiful waterfalls and landscapes as well as to be in touch with the mountains native flora and fauna.

  • Lunch or Snack time at Creole Restaurant.
  • Departure to Trinidad.
  • Stop at farmer house for degustation of exotic tropical fruits.
  • Check in Particular House Trinidad.
  • Dinner in Restaurant.
  • Free Night

    • Optional: Salsa dancing with live music at the House of the Music of Trinidad).

December 23: Trinidad

  • Breakfast.
  • 08:30 City tour Trinidad.

    • One of the first villages founded by the Spanish in the early sixteenth century, declared a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage Site. Visit the Parish Church of the Holy Trinity, with its luxurious altar decorated with precious wood and images of 300 years ago; Plaza Mayor and the Temple House of Yemayá. Visit the Trinidad´s Bodeguita del Medio and bar La Canchánchara to enjoy the traditional cocktail of the same name.

  • Lunch or Snack Time.
  • Swimming and relax at Ancon Beach.
  • Dinner.
  • Free Time

    • Optional: Salsa dancing and performance at House of the Music of Trinidad).

December 24: Christmas Carnival

  • Breakfast.
  • Visit of Point View from “El Valle de los Ingenios”.
  • Visit to the colonial house and tower “Manaca Iznaga”.
  • Transfer to Santa Clara.
  • Lunch or Snack Time.
  • Visit the memorial, museum and mausoleum of Che Guevara.
  • City Tour Santa Clara.

    • Historical city free by Che Guevara in one of the major battles carried out by the revolution forces took place.

  • Transfer to Remedios.
    • Eighth village been founded in the country, its architecture and conservation highlights among its main attractiveness, as well as the biggest Church with its 13 magnificent altars veneered in gold, with a vast carnivalesque tradition and more than a surprise to show.
  • Enjoy the traditional Christmas Special Carnival.
  • Dinner.
  • Night at the carnival.

December: 25 Departure

  • Breakfast.
  • Transfer to Airport.
