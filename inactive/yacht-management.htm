Facts & Figures

The Performance / Active Yacht Ownership Program can dramatically lower costs

over a short time by simply applying these tax laws. An owner can legitimately benefit from substantial tax benefits in order to create equity in their new yacht. We urge potential buyers to seek the advice of qualified tax advisors.

This is a shared revenue program that yields the highest revenue for the owner. While the yield is high, the risk is shared but typically the owner’s share will cover all expenses, mortgage and insurance while the asset can be paid off much quicker. The owner has more choices in the configuration, interior finishes, fabric colors etc. as well as locations.

Owner Benefits

As a Sail Tomorrow Owner you receive these benefits:

Sailing Time

Enjoy up to 10 weeks of sailing* each year on your yacht only. After all you are the owner, so why won't you take full advantage of your asset.

Highest Yield Monthly Revenue

Financially, this is the highest yielding program and a program of choice among many investors. The net income split is 65% to the owner and 35% to Sail Tomorrow.

Operating Expenses

Owners are responsible for all costs including the mortgage payments. When considering a percentage split be sure to pay attention to the bottom-line. Comparable programs at split ratios of for example 80/20, end up yielding less dollars than the Sail Tomorrow's model, because of hidden and extra costs.

Professional Maintenance

Your yacht will be maintained to the most rigorous industry standards. Our team of professionals in accordance with precise guidelines will perform all the work.

Financing

Sail Tomorrow has secured preferred relationships with third party financial institutions in each market. Financing options vary from country to country. We will explain the details and put you in touch with an institution to discuss your financial strategy.

Duration

The yacht can be removed from the fleet at any time with an adequate notice.

Brokerage Support

At the end of the program you may keep your yacht or choose to sell it. We have brokerage offices around the world that will assist in marketing your yacht for resale to assure a seamless transition.

Brands of Yachts & Customization

Beneteau, Jeanneau, Dufour, Lagoon, Fountaine Pajot and Catana. Owner may completely customize layout as well as equipment, but the yacht needs to be charter friendly and easy to maintain. Items like washers, dryers, and dishwashers are not recommended, but the yacht can be “prepared” to have these items installed when the owner leaves the fleet.
