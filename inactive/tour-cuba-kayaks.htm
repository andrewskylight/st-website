Kayaking in Cuba

Sailing and Kayaking - the best of both worlds.

Sail Tomorrow invites you to explore magical Canarreos Archipelago, Cuba. Together we will set sail to world-renowned Cayo Largo, and explore it's surroundings on kayaks.

Tours

Our tours had been crafted to maximize your enjoyment experience. We host tours in two directions. Our first tour heads towards Cayo Largo. While the second one glides along the coast towards Trinidad.

Tour: Cayo Largo

Itinerary:

  • Day 1. Embarkation Cienfuegos, Cuba. Sail to Cayo Guano Del Este.
  • Day 2. Cayo Largo - paddle kayaks.
  • Day 3. Cayo Pedro - paddle kayaks.
  • Day 4. Cayo Alcotraces
  • Day 5. Cayo Rico
  • Day 6. Cayo Guano Del Este
  • Day 7. Debarkation Cienfuegos, Cuba at 07:00am.

Pricing:

1000 €/Pax

Tour: Trinidad

Itinerary:

  • Day 1. Embarkation Cienfuegos, Cuba. Cayo Guano, Cayo Tabaco - kayaking
  • Day 2. Ensenada Palmar - kayaking.
  • Day 3. Cayos Machos De Tierra - kayaking.
  • Day 4. Cayo Chiquito - kayaking.
  • Day 5. Cayo Magua - kayaking
  • Day 6. Cayo Blanco de Casilda - kayaking
  • Day 7. Debarkation Cienfuegos, Cuba at 07:00am.

Pricing:

1000 €/Pax
