Arrange a Night Dive

Fluorescent (Fluo), Night Diving, is a “new” approach to night diving that is becoming very popular. Divers are taking a new look at familiar dive sites in a new light. On a fluo dive, you use a “blue light” to illuminate features of the dive site.

People to People programs

We are happy to announce that we had concluded a partnership with Instituto de Oceanología, Havana, Cuba to bring Cuban marine life experts within arms reach.

Guests are always accompanied by Cuban nationals and engage in exchanging personal experiences and exploring marine life full-time.

Just imagine perceiving life from a point of view of a Cuban taxi driver, who drives 56 Chevy, carries an iPhone, and up until a few years back was not allowed to buy or sell his house.
