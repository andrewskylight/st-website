Meet Phoenix - Bahamian Liveaboard

Experience a different kind of liveaboard on our beautiful yacht, naturally sailing on the sea and breathing the fresh air.

Phoenix is a dream yacht. Built in 2012 by world-class French manufacturer. The yacht was completely renovated in 2018 to meet our guest's sailing and diving needs.

Accommodations Summary:

  • Accommodates 8 passengers and 3 crew members
  • 4 double cabins with private washrooms and showers
  • Stocked kitchen and toiletries on board, with regular cleaning by our crew
  • Dive compressor and tanks on board to refill tanks between dives.
